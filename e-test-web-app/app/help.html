About
About E-Coach Solutions
E-Coach Solutions is an international IT firm whose primary goal is to improve educational outcomes and access by delivering a suite of cutting-edge technology-based products, services and platforms that aid various aspects of the teaching and learning process, especially in the developing world.
About eTEST©
eTest, by E-Coach Solutions, is an application that enables you to take tests on subjects being studied in school. These tests are timed. Scoring of tests is instant. It enables you to compare your performance over time. There are over 10,000 questions under every subject and many more will be coming your way as updates via our website. Updates may include new features as well as new content from time to time. Currently we offering tests based on subjects in Junior High School and Senior High School.
eTest Versions
eTest is available in two versions: eTest Web© and eTest Desktop©.

Both eTest Web© and eTest Desktop© are powered by the same innovative test engine developed by E-Coach Solutions.

Additional Features Available only on eTEST Web®
  • The online version gives you access to a larger test bank ie. more questions.
  • The online version gets updated more frequently. Additional questions are added daily.
  • The online version helps you to synchronize test scores as such test history is never lost.
  • New topics and new subjects are always being added.
